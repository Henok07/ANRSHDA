About Us

Mission: To develop and implement diverse housing strategies, ensuring accessible and sustainable housing solutions for all citizens in the ANRS region.

Vision: A future where every citizen has a home, fostering inclusive urban growth and improved quality of life.

Our Team: A dedicated group of urban planners, engineers, architects, environmental experts, and urban managers, all working together to drive excellence in modern urban development.

Achievements: Successfully built around 1,000,000 houses through both private and government-led initiatives, transforming communities and enhancing living standards across the ANRS region.
